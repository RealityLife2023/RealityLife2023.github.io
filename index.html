Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are there potential risks when relying on Reality Life for personal memory storage?

    There is no risk since we are implementing the necessary cybersecurity and we additionally work with the Blockchain to guarantee security, decentralization and traceability of the data.

  • What is the psychological impact for family members of using Reality Life to talk to their loved ones who are no longer here ?

    We are carrying out studies with specialists (Psychologists, Psychiatrists and Sociologists) to put in place the necessary protections since not all use cases and personalities are the same.

  • What is the importance of transparency and data ownership when trusting companies to preserve our memories through technology?

    Transparency and data ownership are fundamental pillars for users to trust us to preserve their memories through technology, and are essential to guarantee the protection of privacy, security and respect for user autonomy of the platform.

  • How can blockchain technology be used to ensure the integrity and authenticity of stored memories?

    Blockchain can ensure the integrity and authenticity of memories stored by Reality Life technology by providing data immutability, digital signatures, decentralized distribution, and transparent transaction recording. These features strengthen confidence in the authenticity of memories preserved through this innovative technology.
